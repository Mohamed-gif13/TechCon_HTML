History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2005 as a small gathering of innovators and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest technology conferences, attracting thousands of attendees from around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring together the brightest minds in technology to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing. At TechCon 2024, we aim to inspire and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

Throughout the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in technology. Here are a few of our past speakers:

  • Elon Musk - CEO of SpaceX
  • Satya Nadella - CEO of Microsoft
  • Sheryl Sandberg - Former COO of Facebook
